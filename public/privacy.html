⚠️ Important Disclaimer

This program is an independent educational practice tool. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by the Florida Department of Education or the official Florida Statewide Assessments program. All practice questions and instructional materials are original and are designed to support Florida B.E.S.T. Standards-aligned reading and math skill development.

Overview

FAST Track Reading & Math ("we," "our," or "the Service") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and families. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information from parents and students under 13 years old, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Information We Collect

From Parents

  • Account Information: Email address, display name, and password (encrypted)
  • Parent Portal PIN: 4-digit PIN for quick access (stored securely)

From Students (Under 13)

We only collect this information after verifiable parent consent:

  • Profile Information: First name, grade level, avatar emoji/color
  • Educational Progress: Reading quiz scores, math quiz scores, stories completed, daily activity dates
  • Preferences: Favorite stories (stored locally in browser only)
  • Feedback: Optional story ratings (thumbs up/down)

We do NOT collect:

  • Student email addresses or contact information
  • Student photos or voice recordings
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Social security numbers or identification numbers
  • Persistent device identifiers for advertising purposes

How We Use Information

  • Educational Progress Tracking: Record quiz scores, completed stories, and learning achievements
  • Personalization: Show grade-appropriate content and track individual progress
  • Parent Communication: Send password resets and account-related emails to parents only
  • Service Improvement: Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve content and features

We Never:

  • Sell or rent personal information to third parties
  • Use student data for targeted advertising
  • Share student information with third parties (except as required by law)
  • Require students to provide more information than necessary to use the Service

Data Storage and Security

  • Secure Cloud Storage: All data is stored securely using Google Firebase/Firestore with industry-standard encryption
  • Access Controls: Only authenticated parents can access their children's data
  • Data Retention: Student data is retained while the account is active and for 30 days after account deletion
  • Local Storage: Some preferences (like favorites) are stored only in the browser and never uploaded to our servers

Parent Rights (COPPA)

As a parent or legal guardian, you have the right to:

  1. Review: Access your child's personal information at any time through the Parent Portal
  2. Delete: Request deletion of your child's data by removing their profile in the Parent Portal
  3. Refuse Further Collection: Withdraw consent and prevent further collection of your child's information
  4. Opt-Out of Communications: Choose not to receive newsletters or updates

How to Exercise Your Rights:

  • Log in to the Parent Portal to view, edit, or delete student profiles
  • Contact us at the email below for assistance or questions

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database, and hosting (covered by Google's privacy policies and COPPA compliance)

We do not use analytics, advertising networks, or social media plugins that collect student data.

Demo Mode

Users can explore the app in Demo Mode without creating an account. In Demo Mode:

  • No personal information is collected or stored on our servers
  • All data remains local to the user's browser
  • Progress is not saved after the browser session ends
