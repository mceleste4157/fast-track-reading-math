🚀 What's New in FAST Track

Last updated: June 1, 2026

June 1, 2026 — Latest Update
🧊
Streak Freeze — Earned every 7-day streak milestone. If a child misses a day, it auto-applies and saves their streak.
📖
4 Paired Passages — More FAST ELA format practice: Florida Springs, Manatees, Marathon Running, and Florida Hurricanes (50 stories total).
💬
Parent message to child — Leave an encouraging note in Edit; your child sees it as a toast when they log in.
📱
Add to Home Screen — FAST Track can now be installed on phones and tablets for quick app-like access.
🌟
Daily login bonus — Kids earn +10 XP just for showing up each day.
🎮
Vocab Games — Word Match and Fill in the Blank games built from story vocab. Find them in the library nav.
📈
Week-over-week trends — Parent view shows ↑↓ arrows comparing this week's reading and math to last week.
⚠️
Inactivity alerts — Parent portal flags children who haven't logged in for 3+ days, with a Send Reminder button.
📥
CSV export — Admin dashboard can now download all student progress as a spreadsheet.
🏆
Grade completion certificate — A printable certificate appears on the Quest Board when a child finishes every story for their grade.
June 1, 2026 — Earlier
❤️
Story favorites — Kids can heart any story while reading; a Favorites tab in the library shows saved stories instantly.
🔤
Spelling practice — New module that pulls vocabulary words from completed stories and drills spelling with definition clues and hints.
📖
Paired passages — New FAST ELA format: two related texts followed by comparison questions. First paired passage now in the library.
✍️
Writing prompts — After every quiz, kids write a short reflection. Responses are saved and visible to parents in the progress view.
🎯
Adaptive story suggestions — If a child scores below 3 stars, the app recommends a related unread story to try next.
🎉
Weekly goal celebration — Confetti and a banner appear the moment a child hits their weekly reading goal.
📊
Sibling comparison — Parent portal now shows all children side-by-side with XP, level, streak, and weekly progress (appears for families with 2+ children).
⏰
Daily screen time cap — Set a total daily limit across all modules. A friendly "Time's Up" screen appears when the limit is reached.
📅
App schedule — Choose which days of the week the app is available. On off-days, children see a "Come back on [day]" screen.
📈
Quiz score graph — Parent view now includes a color-coded bar chart showing your child's quiz scores over their last 15 attempts.
📧
Weekly email summary — One tap in the Parent Portal generates a pre-filled email with all your children's progress for the week.
🔇
Read-aloud control — Three modes per child: Full, Vocab words only, or Disabled — set from the Edit screen.
📚
47 FAST ELA stories — 7 new Florida B.E.S.T.-aligned stories added, including Seminole history, Henry Flagler, figurative language, bioluminescence, and the first paired passage.
⌨️
Keyboard PIN entry — All PIN screens now accept keyboard input: digits 0–9, Backspace to delete, Escape to cancel.
📦
Suggestion archiving — Admin can archive and restore suggestions to keep the inbox organized.
